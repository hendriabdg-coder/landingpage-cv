Background

How I got here.

I taught myself to code. No bootcamp, no CS degree — just a laptop in Samarinda and a habit of not stopping until the thing actually runs.

I started less than a year ago. Rather than collect tutorials, I picked one hard problem — multi-tenant SaaS — and built it properly: tenant isolation enforced at the query layer, snapshot pricing so historical orders stay honest, 273 tests, and a real deployment behind Nginx and SSL with CI/CD. Not a demo. A system.

Along the way I registered PT Endrich Teknologi Digital, because I wanted the work to stand on its own footing. Since then I've shipped two more products and put the first one up for acquisition.

What I'm looking for now is a remote team where I can do this work alongside people who are better than me. I've proven I can build alone. I'd like to find out how much faster I get when I don't have to.
